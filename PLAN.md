# Fix pricing copy, group mindset shifts, expand Education guide, add global search

## What changes for you

### 1. Profile — remove the "lifetime $29.99" claim

- The Pro upsell row on the Profile tab will stop saying "lifetime" since there is no lifetime plan.
- It will instead show the real plan, e.g. "Unlock 11 tools & 2 guides — from $X.XX/mo" pulled live from the actual subscription pricing.
- Same fix anywhere else "lifetime" wording leaked into upsell copy.

### 2. Mindset Shifts — properly grouped by category

- Cards will be sorted and visually grouped under clear headers: Identity & Purpose, Daily Life, Workplace Culture, Communication, and Relationships.
- Each group gets its own colored section header with an icon and a short one-line description so the categories feel intentional, not random.
- Tapping a card still expands the military → civilian → insight detail like today.

### 3. Education Planning — turned into a real guide, not a checklist

- Rewritten as a step-by-step walkthrough that actually answers questions instead of listing tasks.
- New sections:
  - **Step 1: Know what you've earned** — plain-language explanation of the main federal education benefits available to veterans (post-9/11 education benefit, Montgomery-style benefit, vocational rehab & employment program, survivor/dependent benefits, tuition assistance during service) — what each one covers, who qualifies, and how to request your Certificate of Eligibility. Yellow Ribbon program, what it is who is eligible what it does
  - **Step 2: Pick the right path** — degree vs. trade vs. certificate vs. apprenticeship vs. starting a business, with pros/cons for each.
  - **Step 3: Fund the gap** — how to stack federal aid (FAFSA), scholarships for veterans, employer tuition reimbursement, and state tuition waivers to cover what your federal benefit doesn't.
  - **Step 4: State-level help** — many states add their own tuition waivers, grants, and in-state tuition rules. A prominent button "Open State-by-State Benefits" jumps directly to the State Benefits Finder in the Tools tab.
  - **Step 5: Apply & enroll** — order of operations (COE → school certifying official → FAFSA → housing allowance), common mistakes, and deadlines.
- Every section reads like a short guide paragraph followed by the action items, not just bullets.
- All program names stay generic (no brand partner language).
