# Fix quick stats: connect Tasks & Documents, remove Benefits

**Changes to the Today screen's quick stats bar:**

- **Tasks card** — Tapping it navigates to the Documents & Checklist section (Plan tab) so you can see and manage your tasks
- **Documents card** — Renamed from "Docs Missing" to "Documents Collected". Shows the count of documents you've received or verified (instead of missing count). Tapping it navigates directly to the Documents tracking screen with all statuses (Missing, Requested, Received, Verified)
- **Benefits card** — Removed entirely since it's not useful

**Result:** Two tappable stat cards (Tasks and Documents Collected) that actually take you somewhere meaningful when tapped
