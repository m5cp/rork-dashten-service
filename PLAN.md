# DashTen — Military Transition Planning App (Full Build)

## What is DashTen?

A premium planning, tracking, and education app for military members, veterans, and families preparing for civilian life. Forest green + gold color palette. Follows system light/dark mode. All features unlocked in this build (paywall added later).

**Not affiliated with any government agency. Not legal, medical, or financial advice. A planning and accountability tool only.**

---

## Features

### Onboarding
- Welcome screen with founder story ("I built the tool I wish I had")
- Select your branch (Army, Navy, Air Force, Marines, Space Force, Coast Guard, Guard, Reserve, Spouse/Family, Veteran)
- Select transition timeline (24+ months, 12 months, 6 months, 90 days, separated/retired)
- Choose your goals (employment, school, certification, entrepreneurship, VA health care, disability prep, VR&E, relocation, family readiness, financial reset)
- Non-affiliation disclaimer acknowledgment
- Smooth page-by-page flow with progress dots

### Today Tab
- Countdown to separation/retirement date with animated ring
- Top 3 priority actions for today
- Upcoming deadlines card
- Benefit spotlight card (rotating tips)
- Progress ring showing overall readiness
- "What matters this month" summary card
- Quick-access to recently saved documents

### Roadmap Tab
- Visual timeline by phase (24 months → first year after separation)
- Each phase shows checklist items, milestones, and warnings
- "Do Now / Do Next / Do Later" priority sections
- Tap into any phase to see full detail
- Users can mark items complete, skip, or customize
- Phases auto-highlight based on user's separation date

### Benefits Tab
- 11 benefit categories: Health Care, Disability & Claims Prep, Education & Training, VR&E / Career Reset, Employment & Resume, Housing & Home Loan, Insurance, Family & Dependents, Finances & Budget, Records & Admin, Community & Crisis
- Each category has: plain-English overview, why it matters, eligibility factors, required documents, common mistakes, questions to ask, official-link button, action checklist
- Save/follow/mark started on any category
- Search across all benefits

### Documents Tab
- Smart checklist of common transition documents
- Categories: service records, medical records, evaluations/awards, certifications/transcripts, VA records, employment docs, dependent docs, finance/tax docs
- Status tracking: missing → requested → received → verified
- Reminder system for unresolved documents
- Document count badges

### Profile & Settings
- Display name, transition date, household info
- Notification preferences
- Non-affiliation statement (always visible)
- Source transparency page
- Legal section (privacy, terms, disclaimer)
- App version

### Transition Readiness Score
- Overall score based on completed tasks (not outcome promises)
- Category breakdown: admin, health, education, employment, family, finance, housing
- Visual progress bars per category

### Career & Education Planning
- Resume translation helper (military-to-civilian language prompts)
- Interview prep checklist
- Networking tracker
- GI Bill planning checklist
- VR&E exploration checklist
- Compare paths: college, certification, trade school, apprenticeship, self-employment

### Family & Relocation Planner
- Civilian move checklist
- Spouse employment tasks
- School/childcare planning
- Address change/records transfer

### Financial Reset Planner
- Budget reset checklist
- Emergency fund target
- Income gap planning
- Insurance transition reminders
- Tax document reminders

### Crisis & Support
- Visible "Get Help Now" button with 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
- Direct links to official crisis resources
- Never presented as emergency care or treatment

### Content Cards
- "What I Wish I Knew" founder insight cards
- "Common Mistakes Before Separation" cards
- "Documents People Forget" cards

### Progress & Retention
- Daily next-best-action
- Readiness score updates
- Milestone celebrations (25%, 50%, 75%, 100% per category)
- Encouraging, respectful tone — no guilt or fear language

### Sharing
- Milestone share cards: "Roadmap Started", "50% Ready", "Documents Organized"
- Exportable checklist summary

## Design

- **Palette:** Forest green (`#2D5F2D` / adaptive) as primary accent, gold (`#C9A84C`) for highlights and achievements, warm neutrals for backgrounds
- **Typography:** SF Pro with varied weights — bold titles, semibold headings, regular body
- **Style:** Clean cards with subtle shadows, rounded corners, generous spacing
- **Motion:** Spring animations for progress updates, haptic feedback on completions, symbol effects on milestones
- **Mode:** Follows system (light/dark)
- **No camouflage, insignia, tactical styling, or government-looking UI**
- **Accessibility:** Dynamic Type, VoiceOver labels, 44pt touch targets, color-blind safe

## Compliance & Safety

- Non-affiliation statement on onboarding + profile + about screen
- Source transparency page explaining content is publicly available information
- All benefit info includes "verify with official sources" links
- Crisis resources clearly state app is not emergency care
- No official program names used as feature branding
- No guaranteed outcomes language
- Accessibility: Dynamic Type, VoiceOver, contrast compliance
- Only notification permission requested (when user enables reminders)
