# Polish onboarding: unified dark theme, balanced nav buttons, fit-to-screen, instant paywall

## What will change in onboarding

### Unified dark green theme (welcome → finish)
- Every screen sits on the same deep green gradient — no jarring switch to white halfway through.
- All option cards become translucent white "glass" cards with white headings, white body text, and a soft gold/forest-green accent for the selected state.
- Headlines and subtitles on every question screen become light, easy to read on the dark background.
- The selected option gets a clearly brighter glassy fill plus a glowing forest-green border so the choice is obvious.

### Balanced Back & Continue buttons
- Back stays on the far left, Continue on the far right.
- Both buttons get the same height, same pill shape, same prominent text size.
- Back becomes a clearly visible outlined white pill (not a faded ghost link), Continue stays the filled green pill — equal visual weight, equal tap target.

### Everything fits on screen — no scrolling needed
- Tighter spacing between rows, smaller icon tiles, slightly smaller header on question screens.
- Branch screen stays a 2-column grid; timeline, pain points and goals get compact single-line rows so all options are visible at once on a standard iPhone.
- Full words only — no abbreviations, no trimmed labels, no ellipses. If a label is long it wraps to a second line naturally.
- Welcome screen keeps its hero feel but with tighter vertical rhythm so the three bullets are visible without scrolling.

### Paywall actually appears at the end
- The moment you tap "Enter DashTen" on the final screen, the full-screen paywall now opens directly from the onboarding view before it dismisses — no more disappearing into the background.

## Result
A single cohesive dark-green journey from welcome to finish, with readable text everywhere, equal-weight Back/Continue controls, every option visible without scrolling, and a paywall that reliably shows up at the end.
