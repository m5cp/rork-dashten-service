# Remove broken Next Actions, add slide-to-complete toggles, and add state benefits guidance

**What you'll see change**

- **Remove the broken "Recommended Next Steps" section** on the readiness screen — the buttons there were leading to blank pages, so the whole section is going away.
- **Clearer "mark complete" everywhere.** Across benefits, roadmap, today, and tasks, the small circle checkboxes are being replaced with an obvious slide-style toggle (like the iOS toggles you already know). When you slide it on, the item is marked complete; slide off to uncheck it. Each toggle also gives a soft haptic tap so you feel the action.
- **Everything counts toward your readiness %.** Anything you toggle complete — whether it's a benefit step, a roadmap task, or a checklist item — automatically rolls up into your overall readiness percentage and the category breakdown. No hidden lists.
- **State benefits callout inside every benefit.** Each benefit page (Disability, Healthcare, Education, Home Loan, etc.) gets a new "Your State May Add More" card that explains:
  - Your state likely has additional benefits stacked on top of this federal one (property tax exemptions, tuition waivers, hiring preferences, license discounts, etc.).
  - A button that jumps directly to the State-by-State Benefits guide in the Tools tab.
  - A clear recommendation on who to contact locally — your **State Department of Veterans Affairs**, your **County Veterans Service Officer (CVSO)**, and accredited **VSOs like the American Legion, VFW, or DAV** — with a one-line description of what each can do for you.

**Why this makes the app feel better**

- No more dead-end taps.
- One consistent way to mark anything done — you'll never wonder "did this count?"
- Veterans stop missing money and benefits their state already owes them.
